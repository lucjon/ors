Dehn proposed the Word Problem, etc. in 1911
	Dehn, Max (1911). Über unendliche diskontinuierliche Gruppen, Math. Ann. 71 (1): 116-144

	"Das Identitätsproblem; das Transformationsproblem; das Isomorphieproblem"
	Already had notion of finitely presented groups
		Dyck, Walther (1882). Gruppentheoretische Studien, Math. Ann. 20 (1)
	
	Interested in fundamental groups of topological spaces.

Dehn first stated the Freiheitssatz [MacTutor]
	Rigorously proved by Magnus:
	Magnus, Wilhelm (1930). Über diskontinuierliche Gruppen mit einer definierenden Relation. (Der Freiheitssatz), J. Reine Agnew. Math. 163: 141-165.

Thue first described the word problem for semigroups in 1914
	Thue, Axel (1914). Probleme über Veränderungen von Zeichenreihen nach gegebenen Reglen, Kra. Videnskabs-Selskabets Skrifter. I. Mat. Nat. Kl. 10
	tr. Power, James F. (2013). Problems concerning the transformation of symbol sequences according to given rules, arXiv:1308.5858.

Word problem for cancellative semigroups first (mostly) proved undecidable by Turing in 1950
	Turing, A. M. (1950). The Word Problem in Semi-Groups With Cancellation, Ann. Math. 52 (2): 491-505

Word problem for semigroups first proved unsolvable by Post in 1947
	Post, Emil L. (1947). Recursive unsolvability of a problem of Thue, J. Symbolic Logic 12 (1): 1-11.

	Gurevich, Yuri. (1966). The word problem for certain classes of semigroups, Algebra and Logic 5: 25-35

Word problem for groups proven unsolvable by Novikov (1952), Boone (1957)
	[perhaps]
	Novikov, P. S. (1955). On the algorithmic unsolvability of the word problem in group theory, Trudy Mat. Inst. Steklov 44
	
	Boone, W. W. (1959). The word problem, Ann. Math. 70 (2): 207-265

Word problem for one-relator groups proven soluble by Magnus in 1932
	Magnus, Wilhelm (1932). Das Identitätsproblem für Gruppen mit einer definierenden Relation, Math. Ann. 106 (1): 295-307.

Word problem for one-relator special monoids proven by Adjan in 1960
	Adjan, S. I. (1966). Defining relations and algorithmic problems for groups and semigroups, Proc. Steklov Inst. Math. 85
